Sheet Pan Roast Vegetables

Description

I'd think the title was sufficient.

Ingredients:

  • red vegetable
  • orange vegetable
  • yellow vegetable
  • green vegetable
  • purple vegetable
  • white vegetable
  • mushrooms
  • head of garlic
  • olive oil
  • parmesan
  • balsamic vinegar

Equipment

  • sheet pan
  • parchment paper

Directions:
  1. Place parchment in pan
  2. Heat oven to 400C
  3. Chop first 7 ingredients into bite-sized pieces, toss with olive oil, and dump on pan.
  4. Cut off top 1/4" of garlic, so cloves are exposed. Add to tray. Drizzle with olive oil
  5. Roast for 60-90 minutes, depending on how crispy you like your veggies.
  6. Add balsamic and parmesan for the last 15 minutes.
  7. Serve
