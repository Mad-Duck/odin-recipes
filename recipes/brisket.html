Franklin Brisket

Description

Barbecue is one of God's favorite foods. Good brisket is barbecue's finest barbecue. Preparation is very easy, but quite time consuming.

Ingredients:

  • 3-5lb brisket
  • salt
  • pepper
  • vinegar/water spray
  • smoke

Equipment:

  • butcher's paper or foil
  • smoker with water filled drip pan
  • hardwood suitable for your smoker (alder/maple/hickory/oak/mesquite)

Directions:

  1. Heat smoker to 270-285Â°C
  2. Lightly coat meat with salt & pepper
  3. Place brisket in barbecue for about three hours, checking very INfrequently
  4. If brisket seems dry, spray with vineger/water mix
  5. If bark has formed, wrap brisket in paper and return to oven for another five hours or so
  6. Remove when meat is "bendy"
  7. Let rest until meat can be handled, then slice crosswise in 5 to 10mm strips
  8. Add sauce and serve
