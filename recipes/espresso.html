Espresso

Coffee for grownups.

Ingredients:

  • espresso
  • water

Equipment:

  • coffee grinder
  • espresso machine

Directions:

  1. Preheat machine.
  2. Grind coffee to sand-sized granules.
  3. Add 4 T coffee to basket (for a double), and tamp to 25-30 pounds of pressure.
  4. Lock basket to machine, and dispense coffee into cup, 15-25 seconds, or untill stream "goes pale".
  5. Drink.
